Traditional Italian Lasagna

Freshly made traditional italian lasagna with a five cheese blend, homemade sauce, and a combination of savory beef and sausage

This recipe will BLOW your socks off. It's hard to go wrong with lasagna, but with this one you are sure to have your grandma begging for the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian Sausage
  • Homemade Sauce
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Fresh Basil and Fresh Parsley
  • Whole Milk Ricotta Cheese
  • Nutmeg
  • Whole Milk Mozzarella
  • Provolone, Romano, and Parmesan Cheeses

Steps

  1. Make the sauce.
  2. While the sauce is cooking mix together the cheeses and cook the noodles.
  3. Put the Lasagna together.
  4. Remove the lasagna from the fridge 45 minutes before baking.
  5. Bake at 375 degrees Farenheit for 25 minutes with foil
  6. Remove foil and continue baking for an additional 30 minutes
  7. Remove, slice, and enjoy!
